Military Expenditure from 2007 to 2016

This dataset and information are provided by UNdata and SIPRI. Some information and dataset are from this wikipedia The data from UNdata includes each country's military expenditure(% of GDP and US$) from 1988 to 2016. The information from SIPRI includes the share of military enpenditure in 2017. In this website, I focus on 10 countries, which include China, France, Germany, Great britain, India, Japan ,Russia, South Korea, and U.S.A from 2007 to 2017.
