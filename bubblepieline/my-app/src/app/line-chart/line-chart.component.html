Description and Usage

The Total Trade Value between 1870 and 2014 for the top 10 CINC Score countries is represented in the form of a responsive line chart along with a legend. When the mouse hovers a particular country in the legend, the corresponding country's line pops-out and is highlighted. Also, for each year the corresponding data is shown as a point on the line. On hovering the mouse over the point, the corresponding country and the trade value is shown using a tooltip.
