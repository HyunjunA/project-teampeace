Description and Usage

The Military Personnel Data of the countries is represented in the form of a interactive pie chart. When the mouse hovers over a sector of the pie chart, Military Personnel (in thousands) of that particular country is shown. The top 5 countries in 2012 are China, United States of America, India, Korea and Russia.
