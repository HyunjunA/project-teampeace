Description and Usage

The Composite Index of National Capability (CINC) of the countries is represented in the form of a interactive pie chart. When the mouse hovers over a sector of the pie chart, the CINC Score of that particular country is shown. The top 5 countries in 1945 are United States of America, Russia, China, United Kingdom and Germany.
