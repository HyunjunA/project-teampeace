Slope Graph showing Military Expenditure in 2007 and 2016

The slope graph below shows military expenditures (% of GDP) for 10 countries in 2007 and 2016. The story here is that relative rank or countries according to miliatry expenditures as a percentage of GDP is relatively the same in 2016 compared to 2007. While the U.S. remains the top spender, it has expereinced the greatest decline between 2007 and 2016, while Iran has experienced the greatest increase in the same period.
